Cat Adopt

Cat Adopt is an online store , searchable database of animals who need homes. It is also a directory of nearly 11,000 animal shelters and adoption organizations.

Our mission

    To use Internet technology and the resources it can generate to:
  • Increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality adoptable pets
  • Increase the overall effectiveness of pet adoption programs
  • Elevate the status of pets to that of family member
